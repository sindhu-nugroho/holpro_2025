Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw values intelligence, learning, wisdom, and wit. The house mascot is an eagle. the Head of Ravenclaw House is Filius Flitwick. The dormitories are in Ravenclaw Tower, and students must solve a riddle to gain entry. Ravenclaw corresponds roughly to the element of air.
